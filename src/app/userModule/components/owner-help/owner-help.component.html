How should I login as a Functionhall Owner?

Go to vedikas homepage.In the top right column you can see login dropdown after selecting login dropdown u can see owner login option.select that owner login option then you can enter the credentials regarding owner.

How should I enter the details of a owner?

Go to vedikas homepage.In the top right column you can see List your venue button.After selecting you will be redirect to owner credentials page.if in case owner forgot to login he should login after selecting list your venue.once if he logged in he can enter all the details regarding owner.

How to search for a Particular Functiohall ?

In the vedikas homepage you can see a search box.In the search box you can enter either cityname or functionhall name.There you will find a particular functionhall details.

How to check My Earnings?

As a Owner you can check your earnings by selecting my earnings option in your owner dashboard.

How to check Customer Data?

As a Owner you can check your Customer Data by selecting Customer Data option in your owner dashboard.
